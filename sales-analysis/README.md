# 🛒 Sales Dashboard - Supermarket Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI Sales Dashboard** designed to analyze supermarket sales performance and provide actionable business insights. The dashboard enables users to monitor sales, profitability, product performance, payment methods, and sales trends through dynamic visualizations and filters.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The goal of this dashboard is to help business stakeholders:

- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Track profit and profit margin.
- Identify top-performing products and categories.
- Analyze customer payment preferences.
- Discover monthly and daily sales trends.
- Support data-driven business decisions.
