# ⭐ Customer Feedback Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI Customer Feedback Dashboard** designed to analyze hotel customer satisfaction and feedback. The dashboard provides insights into customer ratings, Net Promoter Score (NPS), visit purposes, feedback categories, and information sources to support service improvement and enhance customer experience.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The dashboard helps hotel management to:

- Monitor overall customer satisfaction.
- Track Net Promoter Score (NPS).
- Identify the main purposes of customer visits.
- Analyze feedback by service category.
- Understand customer demographics.
- Evaluate the effectiveness of marketing channels.
- Improve customer experience through data-driven insights.
