
# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Power BI Sales Dashboard** designed to analyze sales performance across different sales channels, product categories, customer segments, and time periods. The dashboard provides valuable insights to support business decision-making and improve sales performance.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The dashboard enables business users to:

- Monitor total sales performance.
- Compare sales across different channels.
- Analyze quarterly sales trends.
- Evaluate product segment performance.
- Identify high-value customer segments.
- Support strategic sales planning.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel
- Data Modeling

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- 💰 Total Sales
- 🛒 Sales by Sales Channel
- 📈 Quarterly Sales Performance
- 📦 Product Segmentation
- 👥 Customer Segmentation

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- Total Sales KPI Card
- Quarterly Sales Analysis
- Sales Channel Comparison (In-store vs Online)
- Product Segmentation by Year
- Customer Segmentation Dashboard
- Interactive Visualizations
- Dynamic Business Insights

---

## 📊 Business Insights

- Compared sales performance between In-store and Online channels.
- Identified the highest-performing product segments.
- Analyzed customer segmentation to understand purchasing behavior.
- Monitored quarterly sales trends.
- Supported management with data-driven sales decisions.
