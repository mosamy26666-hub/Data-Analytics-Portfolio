# 📦 Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

This project is an end-to-end **Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard** developed using **Power BI**. It provides comprehensive insights into Sales, Product Performance, Logistics, and Procurement operations to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Monitor overall business performance.
* Analyze sales trends and profitability.
* Identify best-selling products.
* Evaluate logistics performance.
* Track procurement efficiency.
* Improve operational decision-making.

---

# 📈 Business Insights

### Executive Dashboard

* Total Sales reached **16M**.
* Generated **5.61M** in profit.
* Average Order Value is **775.66**.
* Sales decreased during the latest period.

### Product Analytics

* A small number of products generate most of the revenue.
* Product demand is concentrated in specific categories.
* Best-selling products were identified to support inventory planning.

### Logistics Analytics

* On-Time Delivery Rate is **20.43%**.
* Late Deliveries account for **79.58%**.
* Shipping performance differs across carriers and shipping modes.

### Procurement Analytics

* Total Procurement Cost reached **50.28K**.
* Average Procurement Lead Time is **18.64 days**.
* Supplier lead times vary significantly.
* Procurement costs fluctuate across time.

---

# 📌 Business Recommendations

* Investigate the decline in sales performance.
* Improve on-time delivery through logistics optimization.
* Increase inventory for best-selling products.
* Optimize supplier selection based on lead time.
* Monitor procurement costs regularly.
* Focus marketing campaigns on high-performing product categories.
